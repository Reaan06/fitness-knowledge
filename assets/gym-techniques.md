# Gym Techniques — Complete Exercise Guide

## 1. Compound Movements (Multi-Joint)

### Lower Body

#### Squat (Sentadilla)
- **Muscles**: Quadriceps, glutes, hamstrings, core
- **Setup**: Bar on upper back, feet shoulder-width
- **Execution**: 
  1. Brace core, chest up
  2. Push hips back and down
  3. Descend until thighs parallel (or below)
  4. Drive through heels to stand
- **Common mistakes**: Knees caving, heels lifting, rounding back
- **Cues**: "Spread the floor", "Chest up", "Sit back"

#### Deadlift (Peso Muerto)
- **Muscles**: Hamstrings, glutes, back, core
- **Setup**: Feet hip-width, bar over mid-foot
- **Execution**:
  1. Hinge at hips, grip bar
  2. Flatten back, engage lats
  3. Drive through floor, keep bar close
  4. Lock out hips at top
- **Common mistakes**: Rounding back, bar drifting forward
- **Cues**: "Push the floor away", "Squeeze glutes at top"

#### Romanian Deadlift (Peso Muerto Rumano)
- **Muscles**: Hamstrings, glutes, lower back
- **Setup**: Standing with barbell
- **Execution**:
  1. Slight knee bend
  2. Hinge at hips, push them back
  3. Lower bar along legs
  4. Feel stretch in hamstrings, then return
- **Common mistakes**: Rounding back, too much knee bend
- **Cues**: "Hips back", "Feel the stretch"

#### Bulgarian Split Squat
- **Muscles**: Quadriceps, glutes, balance
- **Setup**: Rear foot elevated on bench
- **Execution**:
  1. Step forward into lunge position
  2. Lower back knee toward floor
  3. Drive through front heel
  4. Maintain upright torso
- **Common mistakes**: Knee caving, leaning forward
- **Cues**: "Chest up", "Drive through heel"

#### Hip Thrust
- **Muscles**: Glutes, hamstrings
- **Setup**: Upper back on bench, feet flat
- **Execution**:
  1. Drive through heels
  2. Extend hips to full lockout
  3. Squeeze glutes at top
  4. Lower with control
- **Common mistakes**: Hyperextending lower back
- **Cues**: "Chin tucked", "Drive hips up"

### Upper Body

#### Bench Press (Press de Banca)
- **Muscles**: Chest, triceps, front delts
- **Setup**: Retract shoulder blades, arch upper back
- **Execution**:
  1. Unrack with straight arms
  2. Lower to mid-chest
  3. Press up and slightly back
  4. Lock out elbows
- **Common mistakes**: Flaring elbows, bouncing off chest
- **Cues**: "Bend the bar", "Protect your shoulders"

#### Overhead Press (Press Militar)
- **Muscles**: Shoulders, triceps, core
- **Setup**: Bar at collarbone, feet hip-width
- **Execution**:
  1. Brace core, squeeze glutes
  2. Press bar straight up
  3. Move head through at top
  4. Lower with control
- **Common mistakes**: Arching back, pressing forward
- **Cues**: "Bar path straight", "Core tight"

#### Barbell Row (Remo con Barra)
- **Muscles**: Back, biceps, rear delts
- **Setup**: Hip hinge, flat back
- **Execution**:
  1. Pull bar to lower chest/upper abdomen
  2. Squeeze shoulder blades together
  3. Lower with control
- **Common mistakes**: Rounding back, using momentum
- **Cues**: "Elbows back", "Squeeze lats"

#### Pull-Up (Dominada)
- **Muscles**: Lats, biceps, rear delts
- **Setup**: Overhand grip, slightly wider than shoulders
- **Execution**:
  1. Engage lats, pull elbows down
  2. Chin over bar
  3. Lower with control
- **Common mistakes**: Swinging, half reps
- **Cues**: "Pull elbows to pockets"

#### Dip (Fondo)
- **Muscles**: Chest, triceps, front delts
- **Setup**: Arms straight, shoulders down
- **Execution**:
  1. Lower body by bending elbows
  2. Upper arms parallel to floor
  3. Push back to start
- **Common mistakes**: Shrugging shoulders, going too deep
- **Cues**: "Shoulders down", "Lean forward for chest"

## 2. Isolation Movements (Single-Joint)

### Biceps

#### Barbell Curl
- **Muscles**: Biceps brachii
- **Setup**: Standing, underhand grip
- **Execution**:
  1. Curl bar to shoulders
  2. Squeeze biceps at top
  3. Lower with control
- **Common mistakes**: Swinging, leaning back
- **Cues**: "Elbows fixed", "Control the negative"

#### Hammer Curl
- **Muscles**: Brachialis, brachioradialis
- **Setup**: Neutral grip (palms facing each other)
- **Execution**:
  1. Curl with neutral grip
  2. Squeeze at top
  3. Lower slowly
- **Benefits**: Forearm development, grip strength

### Triceps

#### Tricep Pushdown
- **Muscles**: Triceps
- **Setup**: Cable with rope or bar
- **Execution**:
  1. Keep elbows at sides
  2. Extend arms fully
  3. Squeeze triceps
  4. Return with control
- **Common mistakes**: Elbows flaring, using momentum
- **Cues**: "Elbows locked", "Extend fully"

#### Skull Crushers
- **Muscles**: Triceps
- **Setup**: Lying on bench, arms extended
- **Execution**:
  1. Lower bar to forehead
  2. Extend arms back up
  3. Keep upper arms still
- **Common mistakes**: Elbows flaring
- **Cues**: "Upper arms fixed", "Control the weight"

### Shoulders

#### Lateral Raise
- **Muscles**: Medial deltoids
- **Setup**: Standing, dumbbells at sides
- **Execution**:
  1. Raise arms to sides
  2. Slight bend in elbows
  3. Raise to shoulder height
  4. Lower with control
- **Common mistakes**: Using momentum, going too heavy
- **Cues**: "Pour the water", "Control the negative"

#### Face Pull
- **Muscles**: Rear delts, rotator cuff
- **Setup**: Cable at face height, rope attachment
- **Execution**:
  1. Pull rope to face
  2. Separate ends at face
  3. Squeeze rear delts
  4. Return slowly
- **Benefits**: Shoulder health, posture correction

### Legs

#### Leg Extension
- **Muscles**: Quadriceps
- **Setup**: Seated in machine
- **Execution**:
  1. Extend legs fully
  2. Squeeze quads at top
  3. Lower with control
- **Common mistakes**: Using momentum
- **Cues**: "Control the negative"

#### Leg Curl
- **Muscles**: Hamstrings
- **Setup**: Seated or lying in machine
- **Execution**:
  1. Curl legs toward glutes
  2. Squeeze hamstrings
  3. Return with control
- **Common mistakes**: Lifting hips
- **Cues**: "Hips down", "Control the weight"

## 3. Cable & Machine Exercises

### Cable Crossovers
- **Muscles**: Chest, front delts
- **Setup**: Cables at shoulder height
- **Execution**:
  1. Step forward
  2. Bring hands together
  3. Squeeze chest
  4. Return with stretch

### Lat Pulldown
- **Muscles**: Lats, biceps
- **Setup**: Thigh pad secured
- **Execution**:
  1. Pull bar to upper chest
  2. Squeeze lats
  3. Return with control
- **Common mistakes**: Leaning back too far
- **Cues**: "Chest up", "Elbows down"

### Cable Row
- **Muscles**: Back, biceps
- **Setup**: Seated, feet on platform
- **Execution**:
  1. Pull handle to abdomen
  2. Squeeze shoulder blades
  3. Return slowly
- **Common mistakes**: Rounding back
- **Cues**: "Chest up", "Elbows back"

## 4. Dumbbell Exercises

### Chest
- **Dumbbell Press**: Similar to barbell, more range of motion
- **Dumbbell Fly**: Stretch at bottom, squeeze at top
- **Incline Press**: Emphasizes upper chest

### Shoulders
- **Arnold Press**: Rotational movement, all delt heads
- **Reverse Fly**: Rear delts, posture
- **Upright Row**: Side delts, traps (caution with impingement)

### Arms
- **Concentration Curl**: Peak contraction
- **Overhead Extension**: Long head triceps
- **Preacher Curl**: Short head emphasis

## 5. Bodyweight Exercises

### Push Variations
- **Push-up**: Standard, wide, diamond, decline
- **Pike Push-up**: Shoulders emphasis
- **Handstand Push-up**: Advanced shoulders

### Pull Variations
- **Chin-up**: Underhand grip, more biceps
- **Pull-up**: Overhand grip, more lats
- **Muscle-up**: Advanced, transition from pull to push

### Leg Variations
- **Air Squat**: Foundation movement
- **Lunge**: Forward, reverse, walking
- **Pistol Squat**: Single-leg, advanced
- **Bulgarian Split Squat**: Elevated rear foot

### Core Variations
- **Plank**: Front, side
- **Hanging Leg Raise**: Advanced
- **Ab Wheel Rollout**: Anti-extension

## 6. Olympic Lifts (Advanced)

### Clean (Cargada)
- **Movement**: Ground to shoulders
- **Phases**: First pull, second pull, catch
- **Muscles**: Full body, explosive

### Snatch (Arrancada)
- **Movement**: Ground to overhead
- **Phases**: First pull, second pull, catch
- **Muscles**: Full body, maximum power

### Clean & Jerk
- **Movement**: Ground to shoulders, then overhead
- **Phases**: Clean + Jerk (split or power)
- **Muscles**: Full body, maximum power

## 7. Accessory Movements

### For Squat
- **Front Squat**: Quad emphasis
- **Pause Squat**: Bottom position strength
- **Box Squat**: Consistent depth

### For Deadlift
- **Sumo Deadlift**: More hip, less back
- **Deficit Deadlift**: Increased range of motion
- **Rack Pull**: Partial range, overload

### For Bench Press
- **Close-Grip Bench**: Triceps emphasis
- **Pause Bench**: Eliminates bounce
- **Spoto Press**: Hover above chest
