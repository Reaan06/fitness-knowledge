# Functional Training — Complete Guide

## 1. Foundational Movement Patterns

### Push (Empuje)
- **Exercises**: Push-ups, overhead press, chest press
- **Muscles**: Chest, shoulders, triceps
- **Daily application**: Pushing doors, lifting objects overhead
- **Progressions**: Wall push-ups → knee push-ups → full push-ups → decline push-ups

### Pull (Jalón)
- **Exercises**: Rows, pull-ups, lat pulldown
- **Muscles**: Back, biceps, rear delts
- **Daily application**: Opening doors, pulling objects, climbing
- **Progressions**: Band rows → dumbbell rows → barbell rows → pull-ups

### Squat (Sentadilla)
- **Exercises**: Bodyweight squat, goblet squat, front squat
- **Muscles**: Quadriceps, glutes, core
- **Daily application**: Sitting down, picking up objects, getting in/out of cars
- **Progressions**: Box squat → bodyweight → goblet → barbell

### Hip Hinge (Bisagra de Cadera)
- **Exercises**: Deadlift, kettlebell swing, hip thrust
- **Muscles**: Hamstrings, glutes, lower back
- **Daily application**: Bending forward, lifting objects from floor
- **Progressions**: Glute bridge → Romanian deadlift → conventional deadlift

### Lunge (Zancada)
- **Exercises**: Forward lunge, reverse lunge, walking lunge
- **Muscles**: Quadriceps, glutes, balance
- **Daily application**: Walking, climbing stairs, stepping over objects
- **Progressions**: Static lunge → forward lunge → walking lunge → jump lunges

### Rotation (Rotación)
- **Exercises**: Wood chops, Russian twists, cable rotations
- **Muscles**: Obliques, core stability
- **Daily application**: Turning, swinging, throwing
- **Progressions**: Seated → standing → dynamic → resisted

### Carry (Carga)
- **Exercises**: Farmer's walk, suitcase carry, overhead carry
- **Muscles**: Grip, core, full body stability
- **Daily application**: Carrying groceries, luggage, children
- **Progressions**: Light → heavy → unilateral → overhead

## 2. Core Stability

### Anti-Extension
- **Exercises**: Plank, ab wheel rollout, dead bug
- **Purpose**: Prevent lower back arching
- **Cues**: "Brace core", "Ribs down"

### Anti-Lateral Flexion
- **Exercises**: Side plank, suitcase carry, single-arm farmer's walk
- **Purpose**: Prevent side bending
- **Cues**: "Stay tall", "Resist the lean"

### Anti-Rotation
- **Exercises**: Pallof press, single-arm cable press, bird dog
- **Purpose**: Resist unwanted rotation
- **Cues**: "Square hips", "Fight the twist"

### Anti-Flexion
- **Exercises**: Back extension, good morning, Superman
- **Purpose**: Maintain neutral spine under load
- **Cues**: "Flat back", "Squeeze glutes"

## 3. Balance & Proprioception

### Single-Leg Work
- **Exercises**: Single-leg deadlift, pistol squat, single-leg hop
- **Benefits**: Ankle stability, injury prevention
- **Progressions**: Stable → unstable → eyes closed → dynamic

### Unstable Surfaces
- **Equipment**: BOSU ball, wobble board, stability ball
- **Exercises**: Squat on BOSU, single-leg stand, ball planks
- **Benefits**: Joint stability, neural adaptation

### Reactive Training
- **Exercises**: Box jumps, agility ladder, cone drills
- **Benefits**: Coordination, athletic performance
- **Progressions**: Predictable → unpredictable → sport-specific

## 4. Power Development

### Plyometrics (Pliometría)
- **Low intensity**: Jump rope, box steps, small hops
- **Medium intensity**: Squat jumps, lunge jumps, tuck jumps
- **High intensity**: Depth jumps, bounding, single-leg hops
- **Prerequisites**: Master strength first, proper landing mechanics

### Ballistics
- **Exercises**: Kettlebell swing, medicine ball throws, slam balls
- **Purpose**: Explosive hip extension, power transfer
- **Cues**: "Hips snap", "Explosive", "Full extension"

### Speed Work
- **Exercises**: Sprints, agility drills, quick feet
- **Purpose**: Rate of force development
- **Protocol**: Max effort, full recovery

## 5. Movement Flows

### Animal Flow
- **Movements**: Bear crawl, crab walk, beast hold, scorpion
- **Benefits**: Full body coordination, mobility, strength
- **Sequences**: Link movements together

### Yoga Flows
- **Styles**: Vinyasa, Ashtanga, Power Yoga
- **Benefits**: Flexibility, balance, mindfulness
- **Sequences**: Sun salutations, warrior flows

### Gymnastics Rings
- **Movements**: Ring push-ups, ring rows, support hold
- **Benefits**: Upper body stability, strength
- **Progressions**: Low rings → high rings → advanced holds

## 6. Mobility & Flexibility

### Dynamic Mobility
- **Exercises**: Leg swings, arm circles, hip circles
- **Purpose**: Warm-up, range of motion
- **Timing**: Pre-workout

### Static Stretching
- **Technique**: Hold 15-60 seconds
- **Purpose**: Long-term flexibility
- **Timing**: Post-workout

### PNF Stretching
- **Technique**: Contract-relax
- **Benefits**: Most effective for flexibility
- **Requirements**: Partner or equipment

### Foam Rolling
- **Purpose**: Myofascial release, recovery
- **Technique**: Roll slowly, pause on tender spots
- **Duration**: 1-2 minutes per area

## 7. Functional Training Equipment

### Kettlebells
- **Benefits**: Ballistic training, grip strength, conditioning
- **Key exercises**: Swing, clean, press, Turkish get-up
- **Weight selection**: Start light, master form first

### Medicine Balls
- **Benefits**: Power development, conditioning
- **Key exercises**: Slams, throws, rotational work
- **Weight selection**: Light for speed, heavy for power

### Resistance Bands
- **Benefits**: Accommodating resistance, mobility, rehabilitation
- **Key exercises**: Banded squats, pull-aparts, walks
- **Selection**: Different colors for different resistance

### TRX/Suspension Trainer
- **Benefits**: Bodyweight training, core stability
- **Key exercises**: Rows, push-ups, pikes, planks
- **Adjustments**: Length changes difficulty

### Battle Ropes
- **Benefits**: Conditioning, upper body endurance
- **Key exercises**: Waves, slams, circles
- **Duration**: 20-30 second intervals

## 8. Programming Functional Training

### Daily Movement Practice
- **Duration**: 10-15 minutes
- **Frequency**: Daily
- **Content**: Joint circles, movement flows, mobility

### Strength Sessions
- **Duration**: 30-45 minutes
- **Frequency**: 2-3x/week
- **Content**: Compound movements, core stability

### Conditioning
- **Duration**: 15-30 minutes
- **Frequency**: 2-3x/week
- **Content**: Circuit training, interval training

### Recovery
- **Duration**: 10-20 minutes
- **Frequency**: Daily
- **Content**: Stretching, foam rolling, meditation

## 9. Functional Training for Specific Goals

### Weight Loss
- **Focus**: High-intensity circuits, full-body movements
- **Frequency**: 4-5x/week
- **Duration**: 30-45 minutes
- **Intensity**: Moderate to high

### Muscle Building
- **Focus**: Progressive overload, compound movements
- **Frequency**: 4-6x/week
- **Duration**: 45-60 minutes
- **Intensity**: Moderate to high

### Athletic Performance
- **Focus**: Sport-specific movements, power development
- **Frequency**: 3-5x/week
- **Duration**: 60-90 minutes
- **Intensity**: High

### General Health
- **Focus**: Variety, enjoyment, consistency
- **Frequency**: 3-5x/week
- **Duration**: 30-60 minutes
- **Intensity**: Moderate

## 10. Common Functional Training Mistakes

### Mistake 1: Skipping Basics
- **Problem**: Advanced movements before mastering fundamentals
- **Solution**: Master bodyweight movements first

### Mistake 2: Ignoring Mobility
- **Problem**: Tightness limits movement quality
- **Solution**: Daily mobility work

### Mistake 3: Overcomplicating
- **Problem**: Too many exercises, no progression
- **Solution**: Simple, progressive programs

### Mistake 4: Neglecting Strength
- **Problem**: All cardio, no resistance training
- **Solution**: Balance strength and conditioning

### Mistake 5: Poor Recovery
- **Problem**: Training too often, not enough rest
- **Solution**: Program deloads, prioritize sleep
