# Vita Workouts — App-Specific Guide

## 1. Workout Types in Vita

### Strength Training
- **Purpose**: Build muscle, increase strength
- **Equipment**: Barbell, dumbbells, machines
- **Frequency**: 3-5 days/week
- **Duration**: 45-60 minutes

### Cardio
- **Purpose**: Heart health, endurance, fat loss
- **Types**: Running, cycling, swimming, HIIT
- **Frequency**: 2-4 days/week
- **Duration**: 20-60 minutes

### Functional Training
- **Purpose**: Daily movement, injury prevention
- **Types**: Bodyweight, kettlebells, resistance bands
- **Frequency**: 2-3 days/week
- **Duration**: 30-45 minutes

### Flexibility & Mobility
- **Purpose**: Range of motion, recovery, injury prevention
- **Types**: Stretching, yoga, foam rolling
- **Frequency**: Daily
- **Duration**: 10-20 minutes

## 2. Workout Structure in Vita

### Warm-up
- **Duration**: 5-10 minutes

- **Purpose**: Increase blood flow, neural activation

- **Duration**: 30-50 minutes

### Cool-down

- **Purpose**: Recovery, flexibility

## 8. Vita App Best Practices

### Consistency
- **Schedule**: Set regular workout times
- **Reminders**: Enable notifications
- **Habit building**: Start small, build gradually
- **Tracking**: Log every workout

### Progressive Overload
- **Increase**: Weight, reps, or sets
- **Gradually**: 10% per week maximum
- **Track**: Record all changes
- **Adjust**: Based on progress

### Form Quality
- **Priority**: Quality over quantity
- **Technique**: Learn proper form
- **Feedback**: Use mirrors, film yourself
- **Coaching**: Get professional help if needed

### Recovery
- **Rest days**: 1-2 per week
- **Sleep**: 7-9 hours nightly
- **Nutrition**: Adequate protein, carbs
- **Stress**: Manage effectively

## 9. Common Vita User Mistakes

### Mistake 1: No Tracking
- **Problem**: Can't measure progress
- **Solution**: Log every workout
- **Rule**: What gets measured gets managed

### Mistake 2: No Progression
- **Problem**: Stagnation
- **Solution**: Increase weight/reps gradually
- **Rule**: Progressive overload is key

### Mistake 3: Skipping Warm-up
- **Problem**: Injury risk
- **Solution**: 5-10 minutes dynamic warm-up
- **Rule**: Always warm up

### Mistake 4: Ignoring Recovery
- **Problem**: Overtraining
- **Solution**: Rest days, sleep, nutrition
- **Rule**: Recovery is part of training

### Mistake 5: Unrealistic Expectations
- **Problem**: Frustration, quitting
- **Solution**: Set realistic goals, track small wins
- **Rule**: Fitness is a marathon, not sprint
